PHFA is not a fasting tracker.

Personal Health Feedback System that uses intermittent fasting as a primary method to improve metabolic health through measurable progression, adaptive guidance, and sustained motivation.

The app helps users:

Start intermittent fasting
Sustain the habit despite slow progress
See objective improvements in their own body
Stay motivated through real signals, not generic encouragement
2. 💡 Problem Statement
Most users:

Only consider lifestyle change after health warning signals
Struggle to begin fasting consistently
Lose motivation because:
Results are slow
Feedback is unclear
Outcomes vary per individual
Current App Limitations
Existing fasting apps:

Focus on timers and reminders
Provide little to no meaningful feedback
Do not connect:
behavior → physiological change → long-term outcomes
Do not adapt to individual response patterns
👉 Result: Users drop off early.

3. 🧠 Core Insight
Motivation comes from visible, personal progress — not instructions.

Users will continue fasting if they can clearly see:

“My body is changing”
“What I’m doing is working”
“I am getting closer to my goal”

Trend direction
Rate of change
Signal clarity

Show relationships like:

“Longer fasting → higher ketones”
“Late eating → worse sleep”
“Consistent fasting → improved energy”
D. Personalization Over Generalization
Each user experiences:

Different fat loss rates

Different tolerance levels
PHFA adapts based on:

User’s own data patterns
Not population averages
E. Motivation Through Evidence
Instead of:

Gamification

PHFA uses:

Real physiological signals
Trend-based encouragement
Goal projection
